BE AN AVID READER

Jahirul Opi

Reading can help you learn new things and increase your vocabulary, among many other things. Here are seven steps and advice for individuals who want to become voracious readers. They will help you locate fresh, exciting books and reignite your enthusiasm for reading.

1. Start with subjects or genres that you enjoy. You won't like reading about anything if you don't care about it.
2. Find the books you prefer to read.
3. Make a reading place. A fireplace, sound of the rain and candles: might be the perfect reading spot.
4. Skim, until you read, skim.
5. Apply the 50-Page Rule.
6. Create a reading journal.
7. Schedule some reading time.
